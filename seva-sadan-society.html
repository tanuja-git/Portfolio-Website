SEVA SADAN SOCIETY

A digital transformation that reflects and supports Seva Sadan Society's social impact.

Challenge

Seva Sadan Society is a century-old non-profit organization that provides a nurturing & secure environment for underprivileged girls. With the number of donations and the society growing, came the necessity of creating a new website that could clearly show the community what Seva Sadan Society was developing into. The SSS team engaged us to simplify the donation architecture and overhaul their digital experience to elevate their identity, strengthen their connection to donors, and reflect the detail and warmth they put into the girls' lives.

Approach

To better understand the donation flows and ensure we don't compromise any core donation decisions, we started by understand their process and created a site-map. To give SSS's team a clear vision of our thought process, we went ahead and showed them the hi-fidelity wireframes (with thoughtful iterations made internally), so plain boxes don't bother them, and functionality becomes the focus.

We started with remapping the current site.
The site structure clarity, led to easy to navigate flow.

Look and Feel

The project called for a functional design system in modularity and utility but, elevated in warmth and board information. We included Serif and sans serif typography in contrast with one another to emphasize and contribute to the feeling of the brand. From our color palette to typography selections to large imagery, our goal was to improve readability, emphasize focus, and visually mirror the unique facility experiences.

From color palette to typography selections to large imagery – we aimed to improve readability, emphasize focus, and visually mirror the unique facility experiences.

Engaging through storytelling

Stories are what give life to the SSS, and it is way beyond about the organization. We included the story bites across the site to keep it engaging and show SSS's personality.

We included the story bites across the site to keep it engaging and show SSS's personality.

Frictionless Path to Donation

Throughout the site, we blended content and donation option to help donor(s) explore the stories, their impact, and varied donation options. We segmented the donation options to make the selection quick and easy, followed by steps to make donations frictionless.

We segmented the vast list of donation items to make it easy to scan, select and purchase at an instant.

Responsiveness

So that the users of today can embrace the technology of tomorrow, it needs to be reachable on-the-go. That’s why the Seva Sadan Society website is fully responsive.
