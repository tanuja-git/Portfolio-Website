SPOTLIGHT

Designed a vision and experience strategy for a marketing collaboration platform.

Challenge

Spotlight started as a side project with a friend when we noticed a gap in the market for both brands and influencers/creators. For brands, to stay within the budget and deal effectively with give away products. While for influencers/creators, to reduce the time spent on creating concepts for gigs. Knowing the possibility of all the functionality that could go in, we decided to keep it rudimental –simplified version, with core, specific functions for the MVP state. Considering we are our own clients, that was a challenge in itself.

Approach

The platform needed to put users first with a streamlined and intuitive browsing experience. We totally overhauled the platform to establish a service strategy and product experience unique to both brands and influencers/creators.

Onboarding made for intuitive browsing experience.

Scalability, efficiency, and consistency

Efficiency was the core, not only because MVP was in question, but because we knew everything beyond this initial phase would have to fit in at a later stage. Scalability was in the back of our minds from the beginning; that's how the modular design system came in.

Made an extensive and adaptive design system for scale and consistency.

Quick campaign creation

The campaign creation was divided into four short steps. In these steps we reduced the choice-making and provided sample content to take relevant information hence reducing the learning curve to make the campaign creation process quick to get started.

A campaign is quick to create.

Introducing a leeway for both brands and influencers/creators

It goes beyond the primary function of searching, collaborating, and analyzing. We included a process for brands to allot a budget range during campaign creation within which influencers can quote their price along with their concept for the gig. This way, brands stay within the budget, and influencers spend their time effectively.

A proposal – concept and quote, is quick to share for a gig. Resulting in  influencers to spend their time effectively per gig.
Client may see an overview of influencers/creators platform efficiency in an instant.
All the stats of a gig in one place.

An adaptive analytics system showcases social media insights.

To show the essential data stats, we highlighted them visually. While to maintain and update it quickly, we created them as a template.

Templatized visual representation was the key to scannable and scalable data stats

Responsiveness is a bare necessity!

For influencers, the process is mostly done on a tablet or a mobile – that's where the platform shines.
