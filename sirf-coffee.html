SIRF COFFEE

Building a platform to connect a bespoke dating service with their growing community.

Challenge

With the absence of meaningful interactions at the back of thousands of swipes amongst dozens of dating apps, many have now returned to courtship basics of offline approaches – this is where Sirf Coffee comes in. Away from all the online dating fuzz, Sirf Coffee provides personalized guidance in dating for global Indians. With many online applications, they wanted to manage the onboarding well and revamp the site's look. Sirf Coffee approached us to update just that. Straight forward, no fuzz.

Approach

Q&A sessions had already given us a great foundation, so our main focus was on the website. The existing homepage wasn't immediately engaging for users. It failed to demonstrate what the service was and how it worked quickly. Membership package information was getting lost. Furthermore, the bounce rate on the existing signup process was higher than we would have liked. So we recommended and implemented a segmented onboarding process. With storytelling at the forefront, rich imagery entices users to sign up and try this bespoke service.
